Panini recipe:

Panini with cheese and ham

Ingredients:

  • Panini bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Mayonaise
  • Carrot
  • Little tomatoes
  • Salad

Steps:

  1. Cut open the bread in it's length.
  2. Layer a slice of cheese and a slice of ham on top of the bread.
  3. Put the panini in the preheated iron for ~2mins.
  4. Take a plate and put some mayo on it.
  5. Cut the carrot and the tomatoes to your choosing.
  6. Lay them on the plate along with some salad.
  7. Enjoy!
