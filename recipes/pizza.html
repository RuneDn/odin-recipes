Best pizza recipe:

Pizza with salami, ham and mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Rasped cheese
  • Preferred spices
  • Salami
  • Ham
  • Mushrooms

Steps:

  1. Use fork to poke some holes in the dough.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough using the back of a spoon.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celcius.
  4. Cut the salami, ham and mushrooms in pieces of your preffered size.
  5. Spread the pieces over the dough.
  6. Finish by sprinkling the spice(s) and the cheese over the pizza.
  7. Place in the oven for ~ 12mins.
