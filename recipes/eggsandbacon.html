Recipe for eggs with bacon:

Eggs and bacon

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs.
  • Bacon
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Put the 3 eggs in a bowl and cluth them.
  2. Put some butter in the pan and heat it on max.
  3. Throw the pieces of bacon in the pan and lower the heat a bit.
  4. Add the eggs and stir continuously using a fork.
  5. Put the eggs and the bacon on a plate and enjoy.
